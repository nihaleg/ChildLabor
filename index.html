Child Labor

Child labor is the employment of children in factories across the world, and with over 20,000,000
kids being employed, needs to be stopped.

Some of the causes of child labor:

Poverty

Poverty forces children to have to get a job to keep their family alive.

Demand

Factory owners are always looking for cheap labor to cut costs, and children fit this perfectly.

Some of the effects of child labor:

Injuries

Children can easily get injured in their terrible working conditions.

Sickness

Children are exposed to harsh chemicals that can cause sicknesses and even death.

Development

Children don't get an education or receive proper social skills.

Solutions

What can you do?

  • Spread the world about child labor, so that more people are aware of these issues.
  • Donate to organizations like UNICEF, who can stop child labor.
  • Protest against local factories that employ children.
